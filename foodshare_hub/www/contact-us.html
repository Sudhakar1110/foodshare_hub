Frequently Asked Questions

You can donate food by creating a donation entry through our website. Simply go to the "Donate Food" page, login with your credentials, and fill out the donation form with details about the food you wish to donate.
We accept cooked meals, fresh produce, baked goods, dairy products, canned goods, and beverages. All food must be within its expiry date and properly packaged.
Organizations and individuals can request food through our "Request Food" page. Login and fill out the request form with your requirements. Our team will review and match your request with available donations.
To become a volunteer, please contact us via email or phone. We welcome volunteers to help with food pickup and delivery operations.
